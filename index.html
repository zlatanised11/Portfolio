1. GradHub


GradHub, our collaborative project, is a Java-based platform dedicated to supporting underprivileged college students in various aspects of their academic journey.

• Medical Assistance: Through GradHub, students can access on-site medical assistance where doctors can review their medical history, provide diagnoses, and add detailed notes for future reference.
• Job Opportunities: We've integrated a job portal to connect students with local part-time job opportunities, aiding in skill-building and reducing financial stress. Additionally, students can explore full-time job options later on.
• Housing Support: Our platform facilitates off-campus housing requests, offering a list of affordable housing options near college campuses. We provide both temporary and permanent housing solutions to meet students' needs.
• Higher Education Learning: GradHub enables students to continue their education and pursue graduate degrees. Those previously unable to afford further education can now achieve master's degrees, breaking financial barriers to academic advancement.

Together, our team has developed GradHub to provide a comprehensive support system, ensuring that underprivileged students have equal access to essential services, job opportunities, housing support, and higher education opportunities, ultimately fostering their path to success.

3. Stock Price Trend Forecasting Using Machine Learning


Our team spearheaded the development of a fully operational website empowered by Machine Learning capabilities to predict future stock prices. Key highlights of our project include:

• Machine Learning Integration: We seamlessly integrated Machine Learning techniques to forecast stock prices, enhancing the website's functionality and utility.
• Predictive Modeling Techniques: Leveraging multiple regression techniques such as Linear Regression, Random Forests, K Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Elastic Net, and LSTM model, we consolidated various factors to generate accurate predictions of stock prices.
• Linear Regression: Utilizing Linear Regression, we analyzed the relationship between independent variables and stock prices, providing valuable insights into future price trends.
• Random Forests: By employing Random Forests, we harnessed the power of ensemble learning to improve prediction accuracy, capturing complex relationships within stock market data.
• K Nearest Neighbors (KNN): We applied K Nearest Neighbors to identify similar patterns in historical stock data, enabling more informed predictions of future price movements.
• Elastic Net: Incorporating Elastic Net regression, we effectively handled multicollinearity in the dataset, ensuring robust and reliable predictions.
• LSTM Model: Our implementation of Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model facilitated the analysis of sequential data, enabling the website to adapt to dynamic market conditions and make informed predictions.

Through collaborative effort and technical expertise, our team successfully developed a feature-rich website equipped with Machine Learning capabilities, providing users with valuable insights and predictions to aid in their investment decisions.
